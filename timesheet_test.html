Here's a go at creating Ryan's chart programatically, because I think doing data entry work in Illustrator is a waste of time. This is based off of https://github.com/sbstjn/timesheet.js, which appears to do most of the work for me. The rest is formatting. I fully expect that thinking of the term "timesheet" instead of "chart" or "timeline" will be the hardest part of this.
